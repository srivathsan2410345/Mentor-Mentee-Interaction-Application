Top Tips for Mentees

  • ✅ Be honest and open - Mentors are a non-judgmental listening ear.
  • ✅ Be proactive - Decide what you want from the mentoring relationship; utilize a shared agenda.
  • ✅ Be forward-thinking - Think beyond your current issues and focus on long-term aspirations.
  • ✅ Be open-minded - Challenge your ideas and mindsets.
  • ✅ Be reliable - Stick to the plan, be kind, courteous, and communicate.
